# A quiet corner of the web

My personal site — a small rebellion against the profile.

No connection requests. No "thrilled to announce." No algorithm deciding
who sees it. Just one page, written in my own voice, about the problems
I chase and the things I do when I'm not chasing them.

→ **Live:** [_(elai923.github.io)_](https://elai923.github.io/)

---

## What it is

A single, self-contained `index.html`. No frameworks, no build step,
no dependencies to install. Open it in a browser and it simply works.
The only things it reaches out for are fonts, served from Google's CDN.

The design leans on a few ideas worth keeping:

- **A kinetic hero** — the name breathes on its own and bends toward your cursor.
- **Motion that earns its place** — a scroll progress bar, sections that
  surface as you reach them, an anti-grid timeline, a hover that floods each
  row with ink.
- **Honest "endorsements"** instead of inflated ones, and skill bars that
  measure what's true rather than what flatters.
- **Respect for the reader** — it honors `prefers-reduced-motion`, so anyone
  who'd rather sit still gets a calm, static page.

And a handful of things hidden for the curious. They are not listed here.
That would rather defeat the purpose.


## Making it yours

If you've wandered in from elsewhere and want to reshape it into your own
corner, you're welcome to. The copy lives inline in `index.html`, section
by section — change the words, the palette variables at the top of the
`<style>` block, and the placeholder links in the contact section.

Take the ideas. That's what they're for.
